dorado In Publications¶

dorado has been applied in a number of peer-reviewed scientific publications. We provide references to those publications below for users interested in learning more about the application of dorado in various scientific contexts. If you have used dorado in your research, please let us know by opening an issue on the dorado GitHub repository and we’d love to add your publication to this list!

Publication List¶

  • Barile, G., Passalacqua, P., Kwon, S., & Tubino, M. (2024). Controlling factors on water and sediment partitioning at deltaic bifurcations. Authorea Preprints. https://doi.org/10.22541/essoar.173159997.71559823/v1


  • Tull, N., Moodie, A. J., & Passalacqua, P. (2024). River-floodplain connectivity and residence times controlled by topographic bluffs along a backwater transition. Frontiers in Water, 5, 1306481. https://doi.org/10.3389/frwa.2023.1306481


  • Hariharan, J., Wright, K., Moodie, A., Tull, N., & Passalacqua, P. (2023). Impacts of human modifications on material transport in deltas. Earth Surface Dynamics, 11(3), 405-427. https://doi.org/10.5194/esurf-11-405-2023


  • Wright, K., Hariharan, J., Passalacqua, P., Salter, G., & Lamb, M. P. (2022). From grains to plastics: Modeling nourishment patterns and hydraulic sorting of fluvially transported materials in deltas. Journal of Geophysical Research: Earth Surface, 127(11), e2022JF006769. https://doi.org/10.1029/2022JF006769


  • Tull, N., Passalacqua, P., Hassenruck‐Gudipati, H. J., Rahman, S., Wright, K., Hariharan, J., & Mohrig, D. (2022). Bidirectional river‐floodplain connectivity during combined pluvial‐fluvial events. Water Resources Research, 58(3), e2021WR030492. https://doi.org/10.1029/2021WR030492


  • Hariharan, J., Wright, K., & Passalacqua, P. (2020). dorado: A Python package for simulating passive particle transport in shallow-water flows. Journal of Open Source Software, 5(54), 2585. https://doi.org/10.21105/joss.02585
